Bail & Legal Services

Comprehensive solutions tailored to specific court jurisdictions. We ensure rapid documentation, transparent pricing, and legal compliance.

Surety Bonds

The most common form of bail. We post the bond backing the defendant's court attendance, requiring a co-signer and a 10% statutory premium.

Read Details

Property Bonds

Useful for high-value bail when cash liquid assets are limited. The court holds a legal lien against real estate equity to secure appearance.

Read Details

Federal Bonds

Specifically designed for federal district court cases. These involve detailed Nebbia hearings to prove the legitimacy of funding sources.

Read Details

Cash Bonds

If the court orders cash-only release, we facilitate emergency cash logistics, payments, and receipt management on behalf of cosigners.

Read Details

Immigration Bonds

For non-citizens detained by ICE. We structure specialized federal immigration bonds to ensure families remain united during hearings.

Read Details

Transfer Bonds

If a loved one is detained outside your local region or state, we write the bond locally and transfer it to the target county jail.
